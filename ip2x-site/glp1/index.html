Why this matters

The bit nobody talks about.

GLP-1s work. They also accelerate muscle loss in ways most people aren't warned about. Resistance training and consistent activity are the proven antidote — but only if you actually do them.

  • 01
    Studies suggest 25-40% of weight lost on GLP-1s can be muscle, not fat — affecting metabolism, strength and long-term health.
  • 02
    Resistance training during weight loss preserves muscle, protects bone density and supports the metabolism you'll rely on after treatment.
  • 03
    Consistency is harder when you're tired or low on appetite. Our plans are designed for exactly these conditions — and we pay you to keep going.
